“A NOBLE PERSONALITY

“He was not of rank exalted, He was not of noble birth, He was bred among the people In the breast of Mother Earth. He set out to teach the people Freedom, love, equality, To exhort them to resistance; But to flee the penalty Of the prison, whip and gallows, To a foreign land he went.
